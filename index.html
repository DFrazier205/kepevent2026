Our Story

Built on Expertise,
Driven by Excellence

Founded in 2017, KEP Event Planning is a full-service catering and event planning firm serving clients throughout the Washington metropolitan area. We specialize in delivering seamless, high-impact events for corporate, government, association, and private clients.

At the helm is a seasoned hospitality executive with more than 15 years of progressive leadership experience — combining strategic planning expertise with a deep understanding of client service, compliance, and operational excellence.

Our leadership background includes directing multi-million-dollar catering operations with nationally recognized organizations including Aramark and Levy, exceeding quarterly sales goals and building long-term client relationships that drive repeat and referral business.
